SERVICE CONTRACT — CUSTOMER A (CUS_A)
Contract ID: CTR-2025-0041
Effective: 2025-01-01 to 2026-12-31

PARTIES
This ocean freight service contract is between Pacific Line Logistics ("Carrier")
and Customer A Trading Co., Ltd ("Customer", customer code CUS_A).

SCOPE OF SERVICE
Carrier provides FCL ocean freight on the SGN-SIN, SGN-HKG and SGN-LAX trade
lanes. Committed volume: 1,200 TEU per contract year. Equipment: 20GP, 40GP,
40HC containers.

RATES
- SGN-SIN: USD 450 per 40HC
- SGN-HKG: USD 620 per 40HC
- SGN-LAX: USD 2,850 per 40HC
Rates include ocean freight only. THC, documentation and VGM fees are billed
separately per the standard tariff.

SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT
- On-time arrival target: 85% of shipments within 24 hours of ETA.
- If quarterly on-time performance falls below 75%, Customer A is entitled to
  a 5% rebate on ocean freight for that quarter.
- Booking confirmation within 8 business hours of request.

DELAY COMPENSATION
For shipments delayed more than 72 hours beyond ETA due to Carrier-controlled
causes, Customer A receives a credit of 10% of the ocean freight for that
shipment. Delays caused by port congestion, weather, customs or force majeure
are excluded from compensation.

PAYMENT TERMS
Net 30 days from invoice date. Late payment interest: 1.5% per month.

TERMINATION
Either party may terminate with 90 days written notice. Committed volume
shortfall penalty: USD 50 per TEU below commitment.
